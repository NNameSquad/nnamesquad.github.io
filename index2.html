🏠 Home Page

WELCOME TO THE OFFICIAL WEBSITE OF

NoName Squad

Nice, friendly and tolerant community of people from Poland and beyond who like to play games, joke around and generally have a good time. We're open to everyone and welcome anyone, here you can be yourself whoever you are.

Briefly about us:

NoName Squad is a server created on November 11, 2019 for the purpose of testing and learning about the features of discord, at that time we also focused on creating our own proprietary bot (without any goals for the future). Over time, we noticed that our then non-public project began to make a buzz around itself, we decided to take the opportunity and on December 1, 2019 we opened the server under the name "NoName Squad", which has remained with us to this day. The server began to attract quite a bit of attention towards users, and grew relatively quickly. Noticing its potential, we stated to make the server "for friends" an open community, which we are to this day.

What makes us special?

Here are some examples:

  • Custom-made bots for unique features
  • Frequent giveaways: Nitro, PaySafeCard, Steam Keys, server currency, and roles
  • Diverse channels for every interest
  • Advanced server economy and level systems
  • Many rewards for active participation
  • Friendly and helpful staff
  • Open to cooperation and community suggestions

Privacy Policy

How Do We Collect and Process Data?

Our server is governed by Discord's privacy policy, which can be found here: Discord Privacy Policy. We do not collect or store data outside of the Discord platform, except in specific situations where we may store some data directly in the cloud, such as Google Drive, or on personal devices. This includes data shared with us during recruitment processes, contacting us outside of the platform, or other non-descript situations that require it. We keep records of all deleted or edited messages and actions performed on our server by users for moderation and security purposes. Personal Verification We do not store the photos you provide during verification. Your data is processed solely for the purpose of completing the verification. Once the verification process is finalized, only your date of birth is linked to the Mee6 bot and made publicly visible to other server members. The Mee6 bot handles data according to its own privacy policy. You can view Mee6's policy directly on their website. You can remove your date of birth from the bot at any time using the appropriate slash command. Please note, doing so will invalidate your verification status. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us directly. Thank you for your understanding and trust.
