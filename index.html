WELCOME TO THE OFFICIAL WEBSITE OF

NoName Squad

Nice, friendly and tolerant community of people from Poland and beyond who like to play games, joke around and generally have a good time. We're open to everyone and welcome anyone, here you can be yourself whoever you are.

Briefly about us:

NoName Squad is a server created on November 11, 2019 for the purpose of testing and learning about the features of discord, at that time we also focused on creating our own proprietary bot (without any goals for the future). Over time, we noticed that our then non-public project began to make a buzz around itself, we decided to take the opportunity and on December 1, 2019 we opened the server under the name "NoName Squad", which has remained with us to this day. The server began to attract quite a bit of attention towards users, and grew relatively quickly. Noticing its potential, we stated to make the server "for friends" an open community, which we are to this day.

What makes us special?

Here are some examples:

  • Custom-made bots for unique features
  • Frequent giveaways: Nitro, PaySafeCard, Steam Keys, server currency, and roles
  • Diverse channels for every interest
  • Advanced server economy and level systems
  • Many rewards for active participation
  • Friendly and helpful staff
  • Open to cooperation and community suggestions
